💡 Why PRESENT is faster in software but PRIDE wins on FPGA: PRIDE's L0–L3 matrix linear layer uses 16×16 binary matrix operations that require more CPU cycles in software. On FPGA, these operations are parallelised in dedicated logic gates, cutting PRIDE to just 21 cycles vs PRESENT's 31. This is precisely the contribution of Dahiphale et al. (2025).
